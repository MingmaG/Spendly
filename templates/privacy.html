Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password, along with the expense data you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the core functionality of Spendly: storing your expenses, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and letting you sign in to your account. We do not use your data for advertising.

Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and are only accessible to you through your authenticated account. We retain your data for as long as your account remains active, or until you request deletion.

Third Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) solely to operate the application.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the contact details provided on our website.
